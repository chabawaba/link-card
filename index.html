Dung Beetle

Dung beetles are beetles that feed partly or exclusively on dung. A dung beetle can bury dung 250 times heavier than itself in one night.

  1. Kingdom: Animalia
  2. Phylum: Arthropoda
  3. Class: Insecta
  4. Order: Coleoptera
  5. Suborder: Polyphaga
  6. Superfamily: Scarabaeoidea
  7. Family: Scarabidae
